Title: E2E Test: Select 2 items, provide personal data and checkout
Env: https://www.saucedemo.com/

Prequisities: Cypress installed and configured, user is not logged in

1. Open page https://www.saucedemo.com/
2. Provide correct credentials and log in
3. Select 1st product (t-shirt)
4. Selec 2nd product (backpack)
5. Click on Cart icon in the top right corner
6. Click checkout button
7. Fulfill all data (first name, last name, postal code) and click continue
8. Click on Finish button
9. Click on Back Home button
